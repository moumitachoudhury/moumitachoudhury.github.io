I will be joining as a Lecturer at Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology from January 2021.

Previously, I was a full-time research assistant at Cognitive Agents and Interaction Lab (CAIL), University of Dhaka., where I work on Multi-Agent Systems.

Before joining the lab, I was an undergrad at the CSE department, University Of Dhaka. My current research interest includes:

  1. Multi-agent Systems (Coordination and Planning, Multi-agent RL, Game Theory).
  2. AI for Social Impact and Computational Sustainability (Game Theory and RL for social good).
