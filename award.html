What I do in my free time

I work as a volunteer teacher at Shabab Murshid Development Foundation, a non-profit Organization in Dhaka, Bangladesh. My responsibilites include:

  • Teaching and coordinating the math classes for underprivileged children of grade 6 to 10.
  • Working as the coordinator of the annual study tour, cultural program and ensure participation in National Mathematical Olympiad, Bangladesh.
